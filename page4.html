Trek Overview

The Everest Base Camp Trek is a 13-day legendary journey to the foot of the world's highest peak. This challenging trek takes you through stunning Sherpa villages, ancient monasteries, and dramatic Himalayan landscapes, culminating at the iconic Everest Base Camp.

Experience the thrill of standing at Kala Patthar (5,545m) for the best panoramic views of Mount Everest, Lhotse, Nuptse, and the surrounding peaks. This trek is perfect for adventurers seeking an authentic Himalayan experience.

Everest Trek Highlights

  • Reach Everest Base Camp at 5,364 meters
  • Summit Kala Patthar (5,545m) for stunning Everest views
  • Visit Tengboche Monastery, the spiritual heart of Khumbu
  • Experience authentic Sherpa culture & hospitality
  • Scenic flight to/from Lukla - world's most thrilling airport
  • Trek through Sagarmatha National Park (UNESCO World Heritage)

Trip Itinerary (13 Days – Kathmandu to Kathmandu)

Day 01: Arrival in Kathmandu

Hotel/Guest House in Thamel (Tourist-friendly area). Meet your guide and prepare for the trek ahead.

Day 02: Kathmandu to Lukla (2,840m) – Phakding (2,610m)

Fly to Lukla, trek for 2.5 hours. Overnight at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 03: Phakding to Namche Bazaar (3,446m)

6-hour trek through pine forests and suspension bridges. Overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 04: Acclimatization Day at Namche Bazaar

Hike to Everest View Hotel, return to Namche Bazaar. Overnight stay at the same hotel (Full board package).

Day 05: Namche Bazaar to Tengboche (3,867m)

5-hour trek to the famous Tengboche Monastery. Overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 06: Tengboche to Dingboche (4,300m)

5-hour trek through alpine terrain. Overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 07: Acclimatization Day at Dingboche

Short hike to Nagarjun Hill for acclimatization. Overnight stay at the same hotel (Full board package).

Day 08: Dingboche to Lobuche (4,910m)

5-hour trek passing the memorials of climbers. Overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 09: Lobuche to Everest Base Camp (5,360m) – Gorakshep (5,160m)

7-hour trek to reach the legendary Everest Base Camp! Return to Gorakshep for overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 10: Gorak Shep to Kala Patthar (5,545m) – Pheriche

6-hour trek. Early morning hike to Kala Patthar for sunrise views of Everest. Descend to Pheriche. Overnight at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 11: Pheriche to Namche Bazaar

6-hour trek descending to Namche Bazaar. Overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 12: Namche Bazaar to Lukla

5-8 hour trek back to Lukla. Celebrate your achievement! Overnight stay at Tea House (Full board package).

Day 13: Early morning flight from Lukla to Kathmandu

35-minute scenic flight back to Kathmandu. Overnight stay at a hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 14: Departure from Nepal

Airport taxis can be arranged upon request. Safe travels!

Who Is This Trek For?

Trek Difficulty

The Everest Base Camp Trek is challenging and requires good physical fitness and mental determination. Previous high-altitude trekking experience is recommended. The trek involves long days of walking at high altitudes with potential altitude sickness risks.

Best Time to Visit

    • Spring (March–May): Blooming rhododendrons & stable weather
    • Autumn (September–November): Clear skies & crisp mountain views

Safety & Health Concerns

Pre-Trek Preparation

Trekkers should begin preparing their bodies at least six weeks before the trek. Focus on stamina-building and strength training exercises such as long walks, uphill climbs, and trekking with a weighted backpack.

Altitude Sickness & Emergency

Due to the high altitude, trekkers may feel altitude sickness. In case of emergencies, trekkers can exit the trek via helicopters. This is a safety measure we take seriously to ensure your well-being.

Annapurna Base Camp Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Pokhara

Reach Pokhara, the gateway to Annapurna treks. Explore Phewa Lake, local markets, or relax before the trek.

Day 2: Pokhara → Kuli → Ghandruk

Drive to Kuli via Nayapul and trek to Ghandruk, a beautiful Gurung village with mountain views.

Day 3: Ghandruk → Chomrong

Trek through forests and villages with views of Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, and Machhapuchhare.

Day 4–6: Chomrong → Bamboo → Deurali → Annapurna Base Camp

Ascend through dense forests and glacial terrain to reach Annapurna Base Camp, surrounded by towering Himalayan peaks.

Day 7–10: Return via Bamboo → Jhinu → Pokhara → Kathmandu

Descend through the same trail, relax at Jhinu hot springs, and return to Pokhara and Kathmandu.
