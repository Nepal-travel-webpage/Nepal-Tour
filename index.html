Nepal Bound: Your Three-Step Pre-Departure Guide

Preparing well makes all the difference. Follow these essential steps to ensure a smooth, safe, and stress-free Himalayan journey.

01

Passport & Visa

Ensure your passport is valid for at least six months. Most travelers can obtain a Nepal tourist visa on arrival or apply online before departure.

02

Health & Safety

Prepare for high altitude by consulting your doctor, carrying essential medications, and arranging travel insurance that covers trekking activities.

03

Currency & Essentials

Nepalese Rupees are used locally. Carry small denominations, pack layered clothing, trekking gear, and essentials suited for changing mountain weather.
