When is the Best Time to Trek in Nepal?

Nepal offers world-class trekking throughout the year depending on the region. Spring (March–May) and Autumn (September–November) are ideal for Everest and Annapurna Base Camp treks, while Kailash Mansarovar Yatra is best undertaken between May and September.

Do I need a visa to trek in Nepal? +
Yes, most travelers require a Nepal Tourist Visa, which can be obtained on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport or online prior to travel.
What permits are required for Everest and Annapurna treks? +
Everest Base Camp requires Sagarmatha National Park Permit and Khumbu Pasang Lhamu Permit. Annapurna Base Camp requires Annapurna Conservation Area Permit (ACAP).
What should I pack for Everest or Annapurna Base Camp? +
Layered clothing, trekking boots, down jacket, sleeping bag, trekking poles, sun protection, and basic medical supplies are essential.
Is Kailash Mansarovar Yatra physically demanding? +
Yes, the yatra involves high altitude and long walking days. Good physical fitness and medical clearance are strongly recommended.
