ABOUT US

REDEFINING MOUNTAIN TREKKING

Founded by passionate mountaineers in Nepal, our trekking company was created with one clear purpose — to offer safe, authentic, and unforgettable Himalayan trekking experiences.

From classic routes like Everest Base Camp and Annapurna Circuit to remote high-altitude trails, we focus on responsible trekking, proper acclimatization, and deep cultural connection with the mountain communities.

Who We Are

The Nepal Trek Company is a team of dedicated travel professionals, local guides, and adventure enthusiasts committed to delivering exceptional trekking and tour experiences in Nepal. With deep roots in the Himalayas, we bring unparalleled local knowledge, passion, and personalized service to every journey.

MEET THE FOUNDERS

Mr Dilip

Founder & Lead Guide

Hi & Namaste, I'm Dilip. I've climbed Himalayan peaks and led treks across the Indian and Nepal Himalayas, with over 10 years of experience as an outdoor trip leader and Adventure Journey Instructor. I work with international and diverse teams, focusing on safe, meaningful, and well-guided mountain journeys.

I believe in staying a student of the mountains, always learning with each experience. I believe in calm, positive, and hospitable leadership, with strong attention to safety, teamwork, and respect for nature.

I believe every journey has its own beautiful story—let's make your journey unforgettable. We would be happy to host you and share these experiences together.

Kamal Bahadur Magar

Co-Founder & Operations Director

Kamal Bahadur Magar is a highly experienced tourism professional with over 25 years of dedication to the trekking and adventure tourism industry in the Nepal Himalayas. Born and raised in Dhankuta, his journey began from the grassroots level, reflecting strong determination, resilience, and a deep passion for mountain tourism.

Starting from ground zero, Kamal gained hands-on experience across various roles in trekking operations, logistics, and high-altitude trek leadership, steadily building his expertise. With more than 20 years of experience leading treks across the Nepal Himalayas, he has successfully guided countless national and international trekkers, always prioritizing safety, cultural understanding, and authentic mountain experiences.

Through years of hard work, learning, and leadership, Kamal progressed to become the Co-Founder of the company, where he now plays a key role in operations, team development, and ensuring high service standards. His journey from humble beginnings to a leadership position stands as a true testament to his commitment, integrity, and lifelong contribution to Himalayan trekking tourism.
